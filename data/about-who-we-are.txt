About Strategy First International College
Strategy First is a top private international college in Myanmar and a leading higher education institution that offers a wide range of business, management, and Information Technology IT programs in Yangon and Mandalay.

Strategy First was founded in 2010 with the vision of becoming a world-class institution of higher education. Since then, we have grown into one of the most respected colleges in Myanmar, known for our academic excellence, innovative programs, and commitment to student success.

Strategy First’s vision is the number one international college in Myanmar that provides quality education to the masses of Myanmar people for many centuries to come. We wish to be recognized globally for our students’ positive contribution to Myanmar and the world.

Mission
Developing Myanmar individuals and organizations to become globally competitive.

Our Programmes
Strategy First offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional development programs by collaborating with international universities and institutions. The programs are designed to prepare our students for successful careers in their chosen fields, with a strong emphasis on practical skills and hands-on experience.

All of our programs are taught by experienced and dedicated faculty members who are committed to providing our students with an outstanding education. Our faculty members are experts in their fields, with years of experience in industry and academia.

Our Campus
Our campuses are located in the heart of Yangon and Mandalay. The campus features modern facilities and technology, including computer labs, multimedia classrooms, and a library with a wide range of resources.

We also offer a range of extracurricular activities and events, including clubs, sports teams, and cultural events. These activities provide our students with opportunities to develop their leadership, teamwork, and communication skills, and to connect with other students who share their interests and passions.

Our Commitment to Quality
At Strategy First, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic excellence and quality. The programs are designed to meet the needs of our students and the wider community.

We also have a strong commitment to research and innovation. Our faculty members are engaged in developing research projects in their fields, and our students have opportunities to work on research projects and gain hands-on experience in the latest technologies.

Our Community
Strategy First is more than just an institution of higher education; it's a community of students, faculty, staff, and alumni who are committed to making a difference in the world. Our graduates are leaders in their fields, making a positive impact on their communities and the world at large.

We are proud of our diverse and inclusive community, which includes students from all backgrounds and cultures. We believe that diversity is a strength, and we are committed to creating an environment that supports and celebrates all members of our community.
