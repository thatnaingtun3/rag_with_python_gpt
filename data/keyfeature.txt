## **1. ABOUT STRATEGY FIRST**

### **Overview**
Strategy First is a top private international college in Myanmar and a leading higher education institution that offers a wide range of business, management, and Information Technology IT programs in Yangon and Mandalay.

### **History & Establishment**
- **Founded:** 2010 by U Aung Chit Khin
- **2016:** Became an approved learning partner of Heriot-Watt University, offering MBA and MSc programmes
- **2018:** Partnered with Oxford Brookes University for BA (Hons) in Business Management (Top-up) programme
- **2024:** Obtained College status from the Myanmar Investment Commission in September 2024

### **Tagline**
"Ready for the future, Ready for the world"

### **Core Description**
Strategy First was founded with the vision of becoming a world-class institution of higher education. Since establishment, it has grown into one of the most respected colleges in Myanmar, known for academic excellence, innovative programs, and commitment to student success.
